What is ElderWise?

ElderWise is an interactive, engaging program designed to educate seniors on how to recognize, avoid, and report scams and fraud. Our mission is simple: Knowledge = Power.

Did You Know? Older adults face average losses of:

  • Ages 60-69: $666
  • Ages 70-79: $1,000
  • Ages 80+: $1,674

Our Program Covers:

  • Phone Scams
  • Email & Text Scams
  • Online Shopping Scams
  • Tech Support Scams
  • Romance Scams

What to Expect:

  • Friendly, knowledgeable presenters
  • Easy-to-understand examples
  • Real-world red flags to look out for
  • Interactive Q&A sessions
