Empowering Seniors to Outsmart Scammers

Our Mission

At ElderWise, we are dedicated to educating and empowering older adults to recognize, avoid, and report scams and fraud. Seniors are often targeted by scammers, but with the right knowledge, we can help protect their finances and personal information.

Did You Know?

  • Scammers target people aged 60+ more than any other group.
  • Average loss for 60–69: $666
  • Average loss for 70–79: $1,000
  • Average loss for 80+: $1,674

What We Teach

  • Phone Scams: Government impersonation, fake lotteries, urgent requests
  • Email & Text Scams: Phishing messages, fake bank alerts, package fraud
  • Online Shopping Scams: Counterfeit websites, stolen credit card info
  • Tech Support Scams: Fake virus warnings and remote access traps
  • Romance Scams: Social media catfishing and emotional manipulation

How ElderWise Can Help

We offer engaging presentations at senior living communities designed to:

  • Educate residents about common scam tactics
  • Teach practical steps to protect personal information
  • Provide resources for reporting and recovering from fraud
  • Promote digital literacy and confidence in technology
