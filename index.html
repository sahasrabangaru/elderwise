Protecting Seniors from Scams and Fraud

Welcome to ElderWise!

We're a student-led program dedicated to educating and empowering seniors. Our mission is to prevent financial and emotional harm by increasing awareness around scams that disproportionately target older adults.

Our Workshops Cover:

  • Phone Scams: Avoid fake IRS, utility, and relative impersonations.
  • Email & Text Scams: Learn to detect suspicious messages and avoid clicking harmful links.
  • Online Shopping Scams: Shop smarter by identifying fake stores and securing personal data.
  • Tech Support Scams: Understand how fake tech support claims operate and protect your devices.
  • Romance Scams: Recognize the red flags of scammers who exploit emotional trust.

Why It Matters

Older adults are often targeted for larger sums of money, and the statistics are alarming:

  • Ages 60–69: Average loss $666
  • Ages 70–79: Average loss $1,000
  • Ages 80+: Average loss $1,674

How It Works

Our free presentations can be brought directly to your senior center or community! We use easy-to-understand language, real-world examples, and interactive discussions to ensure each participant walks away safer and more confident online and on the phone.
